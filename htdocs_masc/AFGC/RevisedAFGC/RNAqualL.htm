Quality Control of Total RNA and DNase Treatment of Total RNA

This protocol is for those customers choosing to turn in Total RNA (If you choose to turn in total RNA you will need 500ug of total RNA for each sample). If you find that you have genomic DNA contamination; you either have the option of cleaning up the total RNA, in which case expect to lose up to ~50% of the total RNA during cleanup, or you can isolate poly(A)+ RNA.

        Quality control of Total RNA
    To determine if your total RNA is contaminated with genomic DNA use PCR. You will need to design primers to amplify an intergenic region and run the PCR product on an agarose gel, (turn in picture of gel). If you see any product, your samples is contaminated and you may either continue to the next step to clean up the total RNA sample, or you can isolate poly(A)+ RNA.

    DNase Treatment of Total RNA

    **** Qiagen RNeasy Spin Columns are found in RNeasy Mini Kit Cat#: 74104

    **** Use Qiagen RNase- Free DNase set for use with RNeasy/QIAamp Columns Cat#: 79254.

    **For 100ug Total RNA**

  1. Adjust sample volume to 100 ml with RNase-free H2O
  2. Add 350 ml buffer RLT and mix well
  3. Add 250 ml 100% EtOH to the lysate and mix well by pipetting
  4. Apply sample (750 ml) to RNeasy spin Column. Spin 15 sec. at > 10000rpm. Discard flow through.
  5. Add 350 ml buffer RWI to spin column. Spin 15 sec at > 10000rpm to wash. Discard flow through.
  6. In new eppendorf tube, add 10 ml DNaseI stock solution to 70 ml Buffer RDD. Mix by gentle inversion
  7. Pipette DNaseI mix (80 ml) directly onto spin column membrane. Place on bench for 15 min.
  8. Pipette 350 ml buffer RWI onto spin Column. Spin 15 sec., max speed
  9. Place column in 2ml collection tube. Add 500 ml buffer RPE onto spin column. Spin 2 min at max speed
  10. Transfer spin column to new 1.5ml collection tube. Pipette 30 ml RNase-free H2O onto silica membrane. Let sit 1 min. Spin 1 min. to elute
  11. Repeat step 10 with another 30 ml RNase-free H2O.
  12. Quantify RNA. Report A260, A280, A260/A280

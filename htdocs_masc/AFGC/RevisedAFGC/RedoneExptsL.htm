Problems Experienced during Processing Cycle 1 AFGC slides

During the processing of Cycle 1 AFGC experiments, we noted problems with the data from a number of experiments. These experiments were deleted from the SMD and redone. The reasons that experiments were withdrawn from the SMD varied from bad mean intensity scatter plots of the Cy3 and Cy5 channels; high background in either channel; or apparent hybridization gradients. The "Ratios on Arrays" plot (in the SMD see: Display Data/View Data/Ratios on Arrays) in which the Cy5/Cy3 ratio for each spot is plotted by the spot position on the slide was generated by the AFGC informatics group in response to the problems with the apparent hybridization signal. The majority of experiments were rejected based on a non-random distribution of ratios in the "Ratios on Arrays" plot and we believe that the source of this problem was our Scanner. Additional checks of Scanner performance and quality control checks of the data (e.g., the "Ratios on Arrays" plot) have been instituted as a result of these problems. All problematic experiments were redone using the same RNA sample if sufficient RNA was available.

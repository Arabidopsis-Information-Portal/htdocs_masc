Arabidopsis Functional Genomics Consortium
(AFGC)

An NSF-funded collaboration among investigators at four institutions designed to enhance our understanding of gene function by integrating two synergistic approaches:Expression Microarray Analysis and T-DNA Knockouts of Arabidopsis genes.

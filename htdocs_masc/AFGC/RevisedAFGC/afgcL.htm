<H1 ALIGN="CENTER"><i>Arabidopsis</i> Functional Genomics Consortium<br>(AFGC)</H1>

<p align="center">

<!--

<img src="srchweb.gif" align=left width=75 height=75>

-->

An <a href="http://www.nsf.gov">NSF</a>-funded collaboration among investigators

at four institutions designed to enhance our understanding of gene

function by integrating two synergistic approaches: Expression Microarray

Analysis and T-DNA Knockouts of <i>Arabidopsis</i> genes.

</p>
